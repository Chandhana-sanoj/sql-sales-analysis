# Business Questions

This analysis was performed to answer the following business questions:

## Sales Performance
1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. How many total orders were placed?
3. What is the average order value?
4. How many pizzas are sold per order on average?

## Customer Demand Patterns
5. How do total orders vary by day of the week?
6. What are the peak ordering hours during the day?

## Product Performance
7. Which pizza categories contribute the most to sales?
8. Which pizza sizes generate the highest share of revenue?
9. What are the top 5 best-selling pizzas?
10. What are the bottom 5 least-selling pizzas?
